Plan Your Visit

Essential information for experiencing Sikkim's monastic culture

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Sikkim's monasteries is between March and June, and from September to November when the weather is pleasant.

Travel Guidelines

Respect local customs, dress modestly, and always ask for permission before photographing inside monasteries.

Festivals & Events

Time your visit with colorful monastery festivals like Losar, Saga Dawa, and Pang Lhabsol for a unique experience.
